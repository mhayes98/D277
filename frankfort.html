Did you know?

The phrase "dog is man's best friend" was coined by George Graham Vest, a Frankfort native, layer, and United States senator, during closing arguments in court.


"The one absolutely unselfish friend that a man can have in this selfish world, the one that never deserts him and the one that never proves ungrateful or trecherous is his dog"
